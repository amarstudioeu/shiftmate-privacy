ShiftMate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Overview

ShiftMate is a shift tracking app developed by Amar Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles your data.

Data Collection

ShiftMate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter in the app (shifts, expenses, notes, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

What the App Stores Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Shift records (dates, start/end times, hourly rates)
  • Expense records
  • Notes attached to shifts
  • App settings (currency, language, time format)

What the App Does NOT Do

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not send data to any server
  • Does not use analytics or tracking
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not require account registration
  • Does not share data with third parties

PDF Export

When you use the export feature, a PDF file is generated locally on your device. Sharing this file is done through your device's standard sharing system. We have no access to the content of exported files.

Permissions

ShiftMate does not request any sensitive device permissions such as location, camera, microphone, or contacts.

Children's Privacy

ShiftMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this URL. We encourage you to review it periodically.
