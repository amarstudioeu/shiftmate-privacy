ShiftMate — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Overview

ShiftMate is a shift tracking app developed by Amar Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles your data.

Account & Authentication

ShiftMate offers optional Google Sign-In. If you choose to sign in, we receive your Google account email address and display name solely for the purpose of identifying your account and enabling data backup. Signing in is entirely optional — the app works fully without an account.

Google Drive Backup

If you are signed in, ShiftMate can back up your shift data to your personal Google Drive (in the app-specific folder). This data is stored in your own Google account and is not accessible to us. You can delete this data at any time from your Google Drive settings.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Shift records (dates, start/end times, hourly rates)
  • Expense records
  • Notes attached to shifts
  • App settings (currency, language, time format)

Premium Subscriptions

ShiftMate uses RevenueCat to manage premium subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data to verify subscription status. No personal financial information is shared with us. Payments are processed by Google Play. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Promo Codes

When you activate a promo code, the code is verified against our Firebase Firestore database. No personal data is stored during this process — only the usage count of the promo code is updated.

Firebase

ShiftMate uses Google Firebase for authentication and promo code verification. Firebase may collect basic usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

What the App Does NOT Do

  • Does not sell personal data to third parties
  • Does not use advertising or tracking SDKs
  • Does not display advertisements
  • Does not share your shift or expense data with anyone

PDF Export

When you use the export feature, a PDF file is generated locally on your device. Sharing this file is done through your device's standard sharing system. We have no access to the content of exported files.

Permissions

ShiftMate does not request sensitive device permissions such as location, camera, microphone, or contacts.

Children's Privacy

ShiftMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this URL. We encourage you to review it periodically.
